Our Premium Products

Premium Vietnamese spices, carefully selected and processed.

Cinnamon (Cassia)

Known worldwide for its strong aroma, high oil content, and rich flavor. Vietnamese cinnamon is prized for its superior quality and intense taste.

Product Forms:
  • Whole sticks (various lengths)
  • Broken pieces
  • Ground powder
Quality Features:
  • Natural sun-drying process
  • High essential oil content (3-5%)
  • Strong, sweet fragrance
  • Rich reddish-brown color
Applications: Food industry, beverages, bakery products, pharmaceuticals, essential oil extraction.

Star Anise

Vietnam is one of the world's leading origins of star anise. Our star anise is carefully selected for its perfect shape and potent aroma.

Product Grades:
  • Whole star (8 petals)
  • Broken pieces
  • Ground powder
Quality Features:
  • Beautiful star shape
  • Strong natural licorice aroma
  • Clean, well-dried
  • Dark brown color
Applications: Cooking seasonings, essential oil production, traditional medicine, tea blends, liquor flavoring.
